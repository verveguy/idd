## Nature of this dataset

This dataset is an **unofficial, fan-made transcription** of the published rules
into a structured, machine-readable form. It was created solely as a
character-building aid for the _In Darkened Dreams_ play group. It is **not**
affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official product of the game's creators.

No rulebook is redistributed with this project. The data reproduces rules text
only to the extent necessary for the character-builder tool to function.

It is offered on the good-faith understanding that a transformative,
non-commercial, fan-made character-building aid of this kind constitutes fair
use. This is the maintainer's position, not a legal determination, and it does
not transfer any rights in the underlying game to anyone.
